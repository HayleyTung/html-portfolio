The Best K-Drama According to Hayley

My top 3 K-drama of all-time.

Reply 1988

This is my favourite K-drama. It takes you back to that 80's era where good music and good bonding existed. You will find most of the scenes shown in the drama are so relatable that it will make you reminisce those days and chuckle.

Move to Heaven

Move to Heaven offers an intricate balance between the agony of those who have moved on and those who have been left behind.

Signal

It is a detective story, full of tense and unpredictable moments, and it may involve a time loop and time travel.
